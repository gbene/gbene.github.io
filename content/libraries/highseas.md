HighSeas is a Julia package focused on accelerating Sequences of Earthquakes and Aseismic Slips simulations using the Spectral Boundary Integral Method on GPUs.


**Associated manuscript**: Benedetti, G., Heimisson, E. R. (2026, submitted). _HighSeas.jl Accelerating rate-and-state simulations with GPUs_
